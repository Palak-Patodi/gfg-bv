About GFGÃ—BV

GeeksforGeeks Student Chapter at BV is a community of students passionate about computer science and programming. We aim to create a platform for students to learn, grow, and excel in the field of technology.

Our mission is to promote technical education and provide opportunities for students to enhance their skills through various events, workshops, and competitions.

  • Organizing coding competitions and hackathons
  • Conducting workshops on latest technologies
  • Providing mentorship programs for beginners
  • Creating networking opportunities with industry professionals
  • Building a supportive community of tech enthusiasts
